Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the "Partner Shopping" application ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when it connects to your TikTok account to post promotional videos on your behalf.

1. Who this applies to

The App is operated by the owner of the TikTok account it posts to. It is used solely to publish the owner's own promotional videos to the owner's own TikTok account.

2. Information we access

Through TikTok's official API and with your authorization, the App accesses: your basic TikTok profile information (open id, avatar, display name) via the user.info.basic scope, and permission to upload/publish videos to your account via the Content Posting API (video.upload / video.publish).

3. How we use it

We use this access only to (a) confirm which account is authorized and (b) upload and publish videos that you have created. We do not collect, sell, share, or use your data for advertising or profiling.

4. Data storage

The App stores only the access/refresh tokens needed to post on your behalf, kept as encrypted secrets in the account owner's private automation environment (GitHub Actions secrets). No personal data from your followers or viewers is collected or stored.

5. Data sharing

We do not share any information with third parties. Data is transmitted only between the App and TikTok's official API.

6. Data retention & deletion

Tokens are retained only while automated posting is active and are deleted when access is revoked. You may revoke the App's access at any time in your TikTok account settings (Security & permissions → Manage app permissions).

7. Contact

For any privacy request, contact the account owner at the email associated with the TikTok account.
